Why are fruits good? (short article)

Fruits are an essential part of a healthy diet due to their rich nutritional content. They are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which boost the immune system and help protect against diseases. The high fiber content in fruits aids digestion supports heart health, and helps maintain a healthy weight. Additionally, the natural sugars in fruits provide a quick energy boost without the negative effects of processed sugars. Fruits like berries, oranges, and apples are especially rich in vitamin C, which promotes skin health and aids in the absorption of iron. Overall, fruits are a delicious and easy way to improve overall well-being and maintain a balanced diet.
